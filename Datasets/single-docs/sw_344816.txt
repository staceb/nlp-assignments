Montpellier – Méditerranée Airport

Montpellier – Méditerranée Airport or "Aéroport de Montpellier - Méditerranée" , also known as Fréjorgues Airport, is an airport in southern France. It is 7 km east-southeast of Montpellier in Mauguio. The airport carries the 10th largest number of passengers in France (over 1.180 million in 2010). A campus of the "École nationale de l'aviation civile" (French civil aviation university) is also located at the airport.
