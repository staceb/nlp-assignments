Doris Johnson

Doris J. Johnson (born June 23, 1923) is an American former politician. She was born in the state of Washington. 

Johnson served in the Washington House of Representatives as a Democrat from the 16th District, as well as the 8th District. 

A school counselor, Johnson studied at Western Washington State College and earned a master's degree in education. She was raised in Bellingham, Washington. She married Harold Johnson and had a daughter. She lives in Kennewick, Washington.
