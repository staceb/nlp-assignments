The Reagan Diaries

The Reagan Diaries is an edited version of diaries written by Ronald Reagan while he was President of the United States. 

The book is edited by Douglas Brinkley. The unedited version of Reagan's diaries were published in 2009. Ronald Reagan was president for eight years.
