Steve Byrnes

Steven Patrick "Steve" Byrnes (April 14, 1959 â€“ April 21, 2015) was an American television announcer and producer. In 1985, he began his working on auto racing reporting, mostly NASCAR. In 2001, he began working with "NASCAR on Fox" as a pit reporter, and later became the host of Fox Sports 1's studio show "NASCAR Race Hub".

Byrnes was born in Chicago, Illinois and grew up in New Carrolton, Maryland. He was married to Karen Goines from 1993 until his death. They had a son, Bryson.

Byrnes died from head and neck cancer on April 21, 2015 in Fort Mill, South Carolina. He was 56.
