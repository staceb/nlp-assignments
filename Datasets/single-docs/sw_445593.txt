¡Tré!

¡Tré! is the eleventh studio album by American punk rock band, Green Day. "¡Tré!" was released on December 7, 2012. It is the third and last album in the "¡Uno!", "¡Dos!", "¡Tré!" trilogy.

The album featured the single "X-Kid". The album debuted at #13 on the "US Billboard 200" chart and the first-week sales were 58,000 copies in the United States.
