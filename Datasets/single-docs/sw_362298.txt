Paul Burmeister

Paul Burmeister (born March 10, 1971) is an analyst for the NFL Network. He hosts shows like "College Scoreboard", "Path to the Draft" and "Playbook".

Burmeister was an American football quarterback at the University of Iowa. He led his team to the Alamo Bowl. He was a throwing quarterback at the 1994 NFL Scouting Combine.

Before he worked for the NFL Network, Burmeister was a sports anchor for KWWL in Waterloo, Iowa. There, he worked with Rick Coleman and Chris Kolkhorst.
