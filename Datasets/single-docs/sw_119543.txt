Martung

Martung, MÄrtung or Martoong is a highland village and sub tehsil of Shangla District, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Province (former ), Pakistan. The center of Martung (Koozkalai) is 982 meters above sea level. Some parts of Martung are more than 1800 meters above mean sea level. Some of Martung's important villages are Kabalgram, Pishlor, Titwalan, Koozkalai, Manzkalai, Shaga and Karindara.
