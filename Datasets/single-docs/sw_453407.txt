Tom Courtenay

Sir Thomas Daniel "Tom" Courtenay (born 25 February 1937) is a English actor. His career began during the 1960s. He is known for his roles in "The Loneliness of the Long Distance Runner" (1962), "Billy Liar" (1963), "Doctor Zhivago" (1965), "The Dresser" (1983), and in "Last Orders". 

He won a Golden Globe Award for Best Actor in 1983 and a BAFTA TV Award for Best Actor in 1999.
