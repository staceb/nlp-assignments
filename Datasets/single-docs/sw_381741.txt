Estadio Nacional de Costa Rica (2011)

The National Stadium of Costa Rica () is a multi-purpose stadium. It is mostly used for football matches. The stadium is in San Jos√©, Costa Rica. It is the largest stadium in Costa Rica and one of the largest in Central America. It holds about 35,000 people. It is the home ground of the Costa Rica national football team.
The stadium was opened in 2011. It was built in the same place where the old national stadium (built in 1924) was located.

The stadium is the only venue of the 2013 Copa Centroamericana.
