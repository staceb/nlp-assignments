Juniper Cobra 14

Exercise Juniper Cobra 14 (Hebrew: ג'וניפר קוברה 14) or JC14 is a five day military exercise with Israel and the United States. The exercise is there to give training in case of a ballistic missile attack from Iran The exercise began in 2001 and is held once every two years. In 2012 the exercise was cancelled to ease issues with Iran.
