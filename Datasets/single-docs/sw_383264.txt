Mario Kart Arcade GP

Mario Kart Arcade GP is a racing video game released in arcades in October 2005. It was developed by Namco and published by Nintendo. It is the first "Mario Kart" game to be released in arcades. A sequel, "Mario Kart Arcade GP 2", was released in 2007.

In "Mario Kart Arcade GP", players can play as characters from both the "Mario" series and the "Pac-Man" series. There are six stages with four courses each. After completing a stage, the player must put in a coin to continue playing. When the player completes all six stages, a "challenge game" comes up. In the "challenge game," the player has to complete a task under a time limit.
