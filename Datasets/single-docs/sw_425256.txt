Robert Peston

Robert James Kenneth Peston (born 25 April 1960) is a British journalist. In 2005 his biography of Gordon Brown, "Brown's Britain" was published. In 2013, "How Do We Fix This Mess?: The Economic Price of Having it All, and the Route to Lasting Prosperity" was published. On 17 October 2013 he was made the Economics Editor for BBC News.
