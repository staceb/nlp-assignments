Al Ruscio

Alfred Ruscio (June 2, 1924 â€“ November 12, 2013) was an American actor who appeared in many television series and movies. He was known for his roles in "Showgirls", "The Godfather Part III", "MacGyver", "The A-Team", and in "Seinfeld".

Ruscio was born on June 2, 1924 in Salem, Massachusetts to Italian parents. He studied at Salem High School and at the Neighborhood Playhouse. Ruscio was married to Kate Williamson from 1954 until his death in 2013. They had four children. Ruscio died on November 12, 2013 in Los Angeles, California from renal failure, aged 89.
