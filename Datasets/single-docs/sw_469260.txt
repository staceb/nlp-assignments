Pretty Fly (for a White Guy)

"Pretty Fly (for a White Guy)" is the first single off American punk rock band The Offspring's fifth studio album "Americana". The song was released on November 9, 1998. The song also appears on their 2005 "Greatest Hits" album.

"Pretty Fly (for a White Guy)" was one of the most commercially successful singles that was released by the band. It was able to make the charts of over 15 countries and was also able to top the charts in nine of them, including in Australia, where it was four times platinum, and in the United Kingdom.

The song was featured as downloadable content in the 2007 video game, "Rock Band". It was also featured in the 2009 video game, "".
