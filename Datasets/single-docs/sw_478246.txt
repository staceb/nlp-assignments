Delsbo

Delsbo () is a locality in Hudiksvall Municipality in Gävleborg County in Sweden. In 2010, 2,192 people lived there. It is located circa 25 kilometers west of Hudiksvall, near the two lakes of Dellen. The place is known for its assembly of musicians at Delsbo Ancient Farm ("Delsbo Forngård") each year.
