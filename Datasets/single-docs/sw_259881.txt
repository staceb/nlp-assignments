Dig It (The Beatles song)

"Dig It" is a song by The Beatles from their album "Let It Be". The song is credited to Lennon/McCartney/Harrison/Starkey. It is one of the few songs to be credited to all of the Beatles; the others are "Flying" ("Magical Mystery Tour"), "Suzy Parker" ("Let It Be" movie), "12-Bar Original" ("Anthology 2"), "Los Paranoias" ("Anthology 3") and the Beatles' version of "Free as a Bird".
