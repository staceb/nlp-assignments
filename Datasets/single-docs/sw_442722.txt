Louise Post

Louise Lightner Post (born December 7, 1966) is the guitarist for the rock band Veruca Salt. 

In 1993 Post started Veruca Salt with her friend Nina Gordon in Chicago. The band released their first album "American Thighs" in 1994. In 1998 Gordon left the band. The band broke up in 2012. In 2013 the band got back together.
