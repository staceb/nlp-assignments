Ordubad District

Ordubad is a raion of Azerbaijan. It is in the Nakhchivan Autonomous Republic. Ordubad is the capital city and the second largest town in Nakhchivan. In addition Ordubad Raion includes 47 villages and settlements. 

The historic name is Goghtan. This is a Turkic way of saying the Armenian word Vortvat. This means ""the wormy place"". The banks of the Araxes river has many worms. These worms were used to make inks for printing.
