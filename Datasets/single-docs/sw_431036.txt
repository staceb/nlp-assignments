Allan McKeown

Allan John McKeown (21 May 1946 – 24 December 2013) was a British television producer. He was known for producing "Tracey Takes On...". This show starred his wife Tracey Ullman. He won an Emmy Award.

McKeown was born in London. He married Ullman in 1983. They had two children. Their marriage would last until McKeown's death. He lived in Los Angeles. His net worth was £75 million.

McKeown died in Los Angeles, after suffering from prostate cancer.
