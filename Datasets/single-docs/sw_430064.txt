Can't Hold Us

"Can't Hold Us" is a song by American rapper Macklemore and producer Ryan Lewis. It is from their album "The Heist" (2012). The music video is nominated for Best Music Video at the 56th Annual Grammy Awards in 2014. It was released on August 16, 2011. The song also features actress Susannah Wetzel, who sings portions of the chorus alongside Ray Dalton.
