Trapped (Tupac Shakur song)

"Trapped" is a single by American rapper 2Pac. The song was featured on his debut studio album "2Pacalypse Now". The song also features vocals from Shock G.

The song is about police brutality. Near the time that the music video was debuting, Shakur was assaulted by the Oakland Police Department when he swore at them for degrading his name and extending the issuing of a ticket sustained during a jaywalking incident.
