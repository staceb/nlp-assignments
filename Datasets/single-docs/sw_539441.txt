Darrell Hogan

Darrell Trayler Hogan (July 2, 1926 â€“ April 6, 2016) was an American football player. He played from 1949 through 1953 with the Pittsburgh Steelers of the National Football League.

Hogan was born in San Antonio, Texas. He first studied at Baylor University before moving to Trinity University. Hogan was raised in Hot Wells, Texas. He coached high school football and track and field in Texas after his playing days. He was inducted into the Trinity University Athletics Hall of Fame in 2003. 

Hogan died on April 6, 2016 from heart failure in Bandera, Texas, aged 89.
