Don't Cha

"Don't Cha" is a pop song by American girl group the Pussycat Dolls. It was on their self-titled studio record "PCD" in 2005. The song is about a girl teasing a man that she is more attractive than his girlfriend.

The song received positive reviews by most critics. "Don't Cha" got very high on records charts worldwide. It hit #2 on the "Billboard" Hot 100. It sold three million copies in the United States and six million worldwide.
