Jory Prum

Jory Prum (January 31, 1975 – April 22, 2016), also known as Jory K. Prum, was an American audio engineer. He was best known for his work in movie and video games. He was the owner of a recording studio located in the San Francisco Bay Area. He worked many games but most famously "The Walking Dead", "The Wolf Among Us", and "Brütal Legend".

Prum was killed in a motorcycle accident on April 22, 2016 in San Francisco, California, aged 41.
