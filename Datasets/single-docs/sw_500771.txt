Viktor Korshunov

Viktor Ivanovich Korshunov (; 24 November 1929 â€“ 17 April 2015) was a Russian movie and stage actor. His career started in the 1950s. He was awarded the People's Artist of the USSR.

Korshunov was born in Moscow. He married actress Yekaterina Yelanskaya in 1953. She died in July 2013. They had one son, actor Aleksandr Korshunov (born 1954).

Korshunov died after a long illness on 17 April 2015 in Moscow, aged 85.
