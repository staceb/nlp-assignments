My Ruin

My Ruin is a metal band from Los Angeles. The members are Tairrie B and her husband Mick Murphy.

My Ruin was started by Tairrie B in February 1999. "Speak And Destroy" was released on 23rd August 1999. Kerrang! gave the album a good review. In 2000 the band's second album "A Prayer Under Pressure of Violent Anguish" was released. The band's third album was released on 23 October 2003.
