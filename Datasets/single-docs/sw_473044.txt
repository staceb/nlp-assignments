Avicii

Avicii is the stage name of Tim Bergling (born 8 September 1989). He is a Swedish EDM DJ, remixer, and record producer. Avicii ranked 3rd on DJ Magazines annual Top 100 DJs in 2012 and 2013. He is ranked 6th for 2014. Avicii has been nominated twice for a Grammy Award. Once for his work on "Sunshine" with David Guetta in 2012. The second time was for his song "Levels" in 2013. Some of his best known songs are "I Could Be the One" with Nicky Romero, "Wake Me Up", "You Make Me", "Hey Brother" and "Addicted to You".
