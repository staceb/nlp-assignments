He was known for his roles in "Next" (2007) and "Akeelah the Bee" (2005). He also appeared in "Cold Case", "ER", "Friends", "The Suite Life of Zack & Cody", "The Bernie Mac Show", "Still Standing", "Dharma & Greg", "Touched by an Angel", "V.I.P.", "Chicago Hope", "The Simpsons" and "Beverly Hills, 90210".

Ong died from complications of brain cancer in California on June 13, 2017 at the age of 76.
