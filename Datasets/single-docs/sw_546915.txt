Carmen Pereira

Carmen Pereira (1937 â€“ 4 June 2016) was a politician in Guinea-Bissau. She served three days as Acting President in 1984. She was the first woman in this role in Africa and the only one in Guinea-Bissau's history. She had the shortest term as the Acting President, serving only three days in office. 

Pereira died in Bissau on 4 June 2016.
