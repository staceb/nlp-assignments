Pedro Cordero Martin

Pedro Cordero Martin (born January 28, 1972 in Barcelona) is a boccia player from Spain. He has a physical disability: He has cerebral palsy and is a BC2 type athlete. He competed at the 2004 Summer Paralympics. He competed at the 2008 Summer Paralympics. He was part of the Spain team in the BC1-BC2 boccia team game. His team finished third. He competed at the 2012 Summer Paralympics.
