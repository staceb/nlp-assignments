Jean-Jacques Pauvert

Jean-Jacques Pauvert (8 April 1926 - 27 September 2014) was a French publisher. He was known for publishing the work of the Marquis de Sade in the early 1950s and as the first publisher of the "Story of O" (1954). He also published the first edition of Kenneth Anger's "Hollywood Babylone" (1959). Pauvert was born in Paris.

He have also published the first French edition of the "Civil disobedience" of Henry David Thoreau in 1968.

Pauvert died in Toulon, France, aged 88.
