Panna Rittikrai

Panna Rittikrai () (born Krittiya Lardphanna ; 17 February 1961 â€“ 20 July 2014) was a Thai martial arts action choreographer, comedian, movie director, screenwriter and actor. He was the leader of the Muay Thai Stunt team (formerly P. P .N. Stunt Team). His best known movies were "The Bodyguard" (2004), "Born to Fight" (2004), "Ong Bak 2" (2008), "Ong Bak 3" (2010) and "Bangkok Knockout" (2010).

Rittikrai was born in Khon Kaen. He was diagnosed with a brain tumor shortly before his death.

Rittikrai died from kidney and liver failure on 20 July 2014 in Bangkok. He was 53.
