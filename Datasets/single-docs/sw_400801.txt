Harvey Lembeck

Harvey Lembeck (15 April 1923 â€“ 5 January 1982) was an American stage, movie, and television actor. He is famous for playing Cpl. Rocco Barbella on "The Phil Silvers Show" (aka "Sgt. Bilko") in the late 1950s, and the clumsy biker gang leader in the American International Pictures (AIP) beach party movies of the 1960s starring Annette Funicello and Frankie Avalon. He made other appearances on television including two on "The Donna Reed Show". On stage, he appeared in "Stalag 17" (472 performances), and as Sancho Panza on Broadway and in the first national company of "Man of La Mancha".
