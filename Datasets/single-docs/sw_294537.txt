Steve Mizerak

Steve Mizerak (October 12, 1944, in Perth Amboy, New Jersey, USA â€“ May 29, 2006) was an American professional pool player. He was a world champion during the 1970s and early 1980s. He won the U.S. Open four times: each year from 1970 to 1973. He also won the PPPA World Open title in 1982 and 1983. In 1986, he was in the movie "The Color of Money". He is a member of the Billiard Congress of America Hall of Fame.
