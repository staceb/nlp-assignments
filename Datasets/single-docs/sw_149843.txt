Andert-et-Condon

Andert-et-Condon is a commune. It is found in the Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes region in the Ain department in the east of France. In 2009, 336 people lived in the commune. The local economy is mostly based around agriculture and wine. The most important monument in the commune is the "Château d'Andert", a 15th-century castle that is listed as a "monument historique".
