Hubert de Givenchy

Count Hubert James Marcel Taffin de Givenchy (born 21 February 1927) is a French fashion designer. He founded The House of Givenchy in 1952.

He is famous for having designed much of the personal and professional wardrobe of Audrey Hepburn, as well as clothing for clients such as Jacqueline Kennedy. He was named to the International Best Dressed List Hall of Fame in 1970.
