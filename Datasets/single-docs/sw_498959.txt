Kitty Jutbring

Anna Maria Kitty Skura Jutbring (born 8 April 1977) is a Swedish reality television contestant, radio/television host, She became known in Sweden after being a housemate on the second series of "Big Brother" in 2002. After that, she became a radio and television host. She has hosted shows on Sveriges Radio P3 and Sveriges Television. 

Jutbring has been an active supporter of feminism and equal opportunities for women. She has been particularly vocal regarding the situation of young girls in Sweden regarding issues of body consciousness and sex. 

Jutbring has also been an active activist for the new and controversial gender neutral personal pronoun "hen".
