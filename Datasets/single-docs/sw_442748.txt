Otakar Brousek, Sr.

Otakar Brousek (28 September 1924 – 14 March 2014) was a Czech actor and voice actor. He appeared in the movies "Andělská tvář" (2002), "Svatby pana Voka" (1971), and "Můj brácha má prima bráchu" (1975). He also appeared in the television show "F. L. Věk" in 1971. He was born in Krhanice, Czechoslovakia.

Brousek died on 14 March 2014 in Prague, Czech Republic. He was 89 years old. He was outlived by his children, actors Otakar Brousek, Jr. and Jaroslava Brousková.
