Alana Nichols

Alana Nichols (born March 21, 1983) is a skier and wheelchair basketball athlete from the United States. She has a disability. She skied at the 2011 IPC Alpine Skiing World Championships. She was the first skier to finish in the sitting women's downhill race and the Super G race. She was the third skier to finish the Slalom and Giant Slalom.
