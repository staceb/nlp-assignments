Sete de Setembro Square

Praça Sete de Setembro (in English: Sete de Setembro Square, and normally called Praça Sete) is a public square in the center of the city of Belo Horizonte, Minas Gerais, Brazil.

It is located at the intersection of two major roads, "Afonso Pena" and "Amazonas", and by the streets Rio de Janeiro and Carijós.
