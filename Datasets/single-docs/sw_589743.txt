Seen (Winterthur)

Seen is a district in the city of Winterthur in Switzerland. It is District number 3.

The district has these quarters: Waser, Büelwiesen, Waldegg, Ganzenbühl, Sonnenberg, Ricketwil, Oberseen, Gotzenwil, Eidberg, Iberg and Sennhof.

Until 1922 it was a municipality on its own. Then it became part of Winterthur.
