Oscar Núñez

Oscar Núñez (1929 – February 9, 2012) was an Argentine actor. He was known for his work in movie and theater. Some of his best known movie roles were in "Nine Queens" in 2000, "Good Life Delivery" in 2004, and "Un novio para mi mujer" in 2008.

Núñez died from complications of cancer on February 9, 2012, at the age of 83. He was buried at the La Chacarita Cemetery in Buenos Aires.
