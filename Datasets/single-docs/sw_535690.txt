Louise Boyd

Louise Arner Boyd (September 16, 1887 â€“ September 14, 1972) was an American explorer. She explored Greenland and the Arctic. She wrote many lots of articles about her journeys and wrote two successful books. Louise was the first person to fly over the North Pole. She did this in 1955.

Louise was a photographer on the trip over the Arctic Ocean in 1941. The crew on the trip to the Arctic called her the "Ice Woman".

She was born in San Rafael, California. She died in San Francisco.
