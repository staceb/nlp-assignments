Paul Jenkins (actor)

He appeared on many television programs such as "M*A*S*H", "Columbo", "Lou Grant", "Kojak", "The Partridge Family", and in "Starsky and Hutch". He is mostly known for his roles as Professor Parks in "The Waltons" and as Eddie in "Dynasty."

Jenkins was born on August 2, 1938 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. He retired in 2005. Jenkins died on July 1, 2013, following a brief illness in Los Angeles, California, aged 74.
