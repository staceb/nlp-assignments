Self Esteem

"Self Esteem" is a song by the Californian punk rock group The Offspring. It was released as the second single from the album "Smash" (1994). It was also the first major single to be released by the band. The song was a worldwide hit, reaching #1 in Latvia, Norway, and Sweden and was one of the most successful singles released by the band. However, according to frontman Dexter Holland, the song has not been very popular in Japan.
