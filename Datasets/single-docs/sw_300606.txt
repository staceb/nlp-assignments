Sergei Milnichenko

Sergei Grigoryevich Milnichenko (; born on July 13, 1966 in Tomsk, Russian SFSR, USSR) is an Soviet and Russian composer, songwriter, guitarist and record producer.

In the 1990s, Sergei married Larisa Alina. They divorced in the 2000s. They had one child, daughter Rassveta Milnichenko (born in the late 1990s).

In October 2009 he married Natalya Milnichenko.

Between first and second marriages he was in a relationship from 2005 until 2008 with Valeria Kozlova.

Currently he lives with his wife in Moscow, Russia.
