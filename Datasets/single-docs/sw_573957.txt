Hassan Al-Jundi

Mohammed Hassan Al-Jundi (1939 â€“ 25 February 2017) was a Moroccan author, movie director and movie actor.

Al Jundi was best known for his role as Abu Jahl, Amr ibn Hisham in the Arabic version of the movie "The Message" and also acted as Kisra in the English language version of the movie. He was also popular the role of Rustam in the movie "Qadisiyah".

He acted as Utbah ibn Rabiah in the 2012's in the historical drama series "Umar ibn al-Khattab".

Al-Jundi died on 25 February 2017 in Marrakesh, Morocco, aged 78.
