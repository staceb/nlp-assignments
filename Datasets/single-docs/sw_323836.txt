Kara Tointon

Kara Louise Tointon (born 5 August 1983) is a British actress. She was born and raised in Essex. She is best known for her role from 2005-2009 as Dawn Swann in "EastEnders". She and Artem Chigvintsev won the 2010 series of "Strictly Come Dancing". Her younger sister Hannah is also an actress.
