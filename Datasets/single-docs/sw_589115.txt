Elsa Daniel

Elsa Daniel born Elsa Nilda Gómez Scapatti (13 November 1936 – 25 June 2017) was an Argentine movie actress. She was born in San Lorenzo, Santa Fe, Argentina. Her career began in the 1954 classic "The Grandfather" and made some 30 appearances in movies between then and 1987. She retired from cinema in 1987.

Daniel died on 25 June 2017 in Buenos Aires, Argentina of complications from pneumonia at the age of 80.
