Ric Suggitt

Ric "Sluggo" Suggitt (October 30, 1958 June 27, 2017) was a Canadian rugby union footballer and a rugby union coach. Suggitt was also the Head Coach of the USA Women's 7's Olympic team. He was the head coach at university of Lethbridge. He was born in Edmonton, Alberta.

Suggitt was the coach of the female Canada national team and the men's national seven-a-side team, when he was appointed to the post of coach of the male Canada national rugby union team, in 2004. 

Suggitt died on June 27, 2017 in Calgary, Alberta from a sudden heart attack, aged 58.
