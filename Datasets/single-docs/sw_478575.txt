Jönköpings IK

Jönköpings IK is a floorball club in Jönköping, Sweden. it was established on 9 March 1985 after a merger of the SMU Immanuel Church and the Munksjö School teams. The men's team has played several seasons in the Swedish top division. The men's team lost the Swedish national finals in the years of 1986, 1988 and 1990. Also, the men's team earned the national championship bronze medals in the years of 1987, 1989 and 1991.
