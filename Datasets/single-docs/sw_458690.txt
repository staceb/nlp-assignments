Monty Meares Wyche (December 4, 1926â€“July 28, 2014) was an American judge. He was a judge of the Louisiana 26th Judicial District Court based in Benton. He served from 1969 to 1988. 

Wyche had jurisdiction in both Bossier and Webster parishes in the northwestern portion of his state.

Wyche died in Shreveport, Louisiana from a long-illness, aged 86.
