Beverly D'Angelo

Beverly Heather D'Angelo (born November 15, 1951) is an American actress. She acts mostly in movies. She first became well known in the 1983 movie "National Lampoon's Vacation". She also acted in many other movies, particularly comedy movies. She was born in Columbus, Ohio.

D'Angelo was in the 1980 movie "Coal Miner's Daughter". She shared the Country Music Association award for Album of the Year for that movie's soundtrack.
