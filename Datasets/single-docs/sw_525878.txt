Prayer in C

"Prayer in C" is a song by the band Lilly Wood & the Prick. It was originally released as a folk-pop song. The song was later remixed by DJ Robin Schulz in 2014. Schulz' remix peaked at number one on "Billboard"'s Hot Dance/Electronic Songs chart. It hit number one in many European countries including the UK and Belgium. It reached the top twenty in Canada. The remix is a dance and house song. It was included on Schulz' album, "Prayer".
