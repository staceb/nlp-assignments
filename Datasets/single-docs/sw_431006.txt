Rap God

"Rap God" is a 2013 hip hop song by the American rapper Eminem. It is the third single from his eighth studio album "The Marshall Mathers LP 2" which was released on November 5, 2013.

The song has been praised for Eminem's rapping speed and his lyrical ability.

It was ranked #14 on Complex's list of the 50 best songs of 2013 and it was ranked #15 on Rolling Stone's list of the 100 best songs of 2013.
