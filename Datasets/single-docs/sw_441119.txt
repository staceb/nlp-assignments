Connected

"Connected" is an electronic pop song by Stereo MCs. This song was released in September 1992. It was #20 on "Billboard" Hot 100 and #18 on UK Top 75 Chart. The song appeared in several television programs and movies.

The song samples the disco classic song "Let Me (Let Me be Your Lover)" by Jimmy Bo Horne. The full length of the song is five minutes and 16 seconds.
