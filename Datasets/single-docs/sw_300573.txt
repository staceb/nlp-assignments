Kelly Rutherford

Kelly Deane Melissa Rutherford (born on November 6, 1968 in Elizabethtown, Kentucky, U.S.) is an American actress.

Kelly was married to Carlos Tarajano from 2001 to 2002.

From 2006 to 2010 she was married to Daniel Giersch. They have two children together — a son Hermés Gustaf Daniel Giersch (b.October 18, 2006) and a daughter Helena Grace Rutherford-Giersch (b.June 8, 2009).

Currently she lives in Manhattan (New York, U.S.) with her children.
