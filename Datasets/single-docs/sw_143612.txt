French Beach, Karachi

French Beach of Karachi is a small fishing village. It is half way between Hawkes Bay and Paradise Point. The local people call it "Haji Abdullah Goth". It is surrounded by a boundary wall. It has 20 huts for rent. The village does not have running water or electric power. Its rocky beach and clear waters are ideal for snorkeling and scuba diving. Visitors must bring their own equipment as well as food and drink supplies.
