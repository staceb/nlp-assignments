Cryptic Writings

Cryptic Writings is the seventh studio album by American thrash metal band, Megadeth. "Cryptic Writings" was released on June 17, 1997 through Capitol Records. It is Megadeth's last album to feature drummer Nick Menza.

The album peaked at #10 on the "Billboard" 200 and at #17 on the Canadian Albums Chart. In 1998, the song "Trust" was nominated for a Grammy Award in the category of Best Metal Performance.
