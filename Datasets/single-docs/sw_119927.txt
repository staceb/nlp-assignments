Losing Grip

Losing Grip is a 2003 single by Avril Lavigne. It is the fourth single from her first studio album "Let Go".

On the "Billboard" charts, "Losing Grip" was able to peak at #17 on the Mainstream Top 40, #33 on the Adult Top 40, and at #64 on the Hot 100 charts. At the 2004 Grammy Awards, "Losing Grip" was nominated for Best Female Rock Vocal Performance.
