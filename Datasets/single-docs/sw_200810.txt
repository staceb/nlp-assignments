Ford Laser

The Ford Laser was an automobile sold by Ford Motor Company between 1981 and 2003. 
It was offered in four bodystyles; a three-door hatchback, five-door hatchback, four-door saloon and five-door estate.

This was primarily sold in the Oceania region of the world. However, some were sold in Latin America, and these were versions of the Mazda 323 sold under the Ford Laser name.
