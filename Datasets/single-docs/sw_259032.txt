Buick Enclave

The Buick Enclave is a full-sized luxury SUV that is built by General Motors's Buick brand. The Buick was first shown at the 2006 North American International Auto Show. The Buick won the Best New SUV / CUV (over C$60,000) award from the AJAC in 2008. The Enclave replaced the Terraza minivan, the Rainier SUV and the mid-sized Rendezvous CUV. The Enclave's styling is based off the Buick Centieme concept crossover
