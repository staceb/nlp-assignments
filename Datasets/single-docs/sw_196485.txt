D. Train

D. Train is a R&B post-disco band from the New York, United States. They were formed in 1980. In band there is James "D-Train" Williams and Hubert Eaves III. Hubert plays on keyboard, bass guitar and drums, James uses vocals as a musical instrument. This duo have some hits on "Billboard Dance" & "Billboard R&B" charts. Their songs include "Keep On" (1982), "Walk on By" (1982), "You're The One For Me" (1982).

They also did the song named "Pokérap" from Pokémon soundtrack album "2BA Master".
