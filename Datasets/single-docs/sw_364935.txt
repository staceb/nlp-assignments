Mike Greenberg

Mike Greenberg (born August 6, 1967) is a television personality with ESPN. He has hosted "SportsCenter", "ESPN Radio SportsCenter", and "Mike and Mike in the Morning" with Mike Golic. He also hosted the game show "Duel" on the ABC Network. He worked as a play-by-play announcer, with Golic, on ESPN's coverage of the Arena Football League. From 2007-2009, Golic and Greenberg worked as the #2 announcers for Monday Night Football. They were replaced by Brad Nessler and Trent Dilfer. They continue to anchor "SportsCenter" and host their radio show "Mike and Mike in the Morning". Greenberg was also an anchor on "ESPNEWS".

Greenberg was born in New York City.
