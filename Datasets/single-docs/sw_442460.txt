Plastic Surgery Disasters

Plastic Surgery Disasters is the second studio album by American hardcore punk band, Dead Kennedys. "Plastic Surgery Disasters" was released in November 1982. 

The album was reissued with their last EP "In God We Trust, Inc." which are the last eight songs on the CD. The cover photo of the album is a picture called "Hands" by photographer Michael Wells. Allmusic rated the album 4.5 out of 5 stars.
