Raquel Alessi

Raquel Nazzarena Alessi (born in Los Angeles, California on March 7, 1983) is an American actress. In summer 1989, in the "Kidsongs" VHS series, she replaced Divita Wright. She appeared in "Kidsongs: A Day at Camp" in 1989. She appeared with Kenny Ford, Jr. on "Kidsongs: Ride the Roller Coaster" in 1990. She had a cameo appearance in "Kidsongs: Very Silly Songs" in 1990. She left the VHS series in 1990. She was replaced by Ashley Nation in 1992.
