Death Before Dishonour

Death Before Dishonour is the fifth studio album by Scottish punk rock band, The Exploited. "Death Before Dishonour" was released on April 15, 1987. The album was re-released on June 19, 2001. The single "Sexual Favours" had a music video released for it. The album was ranked in the top 200 of the Britain Alternative Music list. The re-release of the album featured seven more tracks.
