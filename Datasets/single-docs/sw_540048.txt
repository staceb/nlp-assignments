Punggol MRT/LRT Station

Punggol MRT/LRT Station (NE17/PTC) is an MRT and LRT Station in Singapore. It is an interchange station between North East MRT Line and Punggol LRT Line. These are connected with MRT/LRT stations around the Punggol area. 

The station opened on 20 June 2003. It is the terminus station along the North East MRT Line. Waterway Point is located nearby.
