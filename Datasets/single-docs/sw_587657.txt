Travis Fryman

David Travis Fryman (born March 25, 1969) played Major League Baseball from 1990 to 2002. He played for the Detroit Tigers (1990–1997) and for the Cleveland Indians from 1998 to 2002). He usually played third base, but he also played shortstop. Fryman made 5 All–Star teams and won a Gold Glove Award in 2000.
