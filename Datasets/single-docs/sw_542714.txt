François Maistre

François Maistre (14 May 1925 – 16 May 2016) was a French, television and theatre actor. He was born in Demigny, Saône-et-Loire, France. He appeared in nearly 100 movies between 1960 until his retirement in 2003. He was known for his roles in "Angélique, Marquise des Anges" and "The Discreet Charm of the Bourgeoisie".

Maistre died in France on 17 May 2016, aged 91.
