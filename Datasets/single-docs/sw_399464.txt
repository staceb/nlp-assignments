Elmo Lincoln

Elmo Lincoln (Otto Elmo Linkenhelt, February 6, 1889June 27, 1952) was an American movie actor. He played the first movie Tarzan in "Tarzan of the Apes" (1918). He played Tarzan in two other movies, "The Romance of Tarzan" (1918) and the movie serial "The Adventures of Tarzan" (1921). He appeared, uncredited, in two Tarzan movies in the 1940s. He was a circus roustabout in "Tarzan's New York Adventure" (1942), and a fisherman repairing his net in "Tarzan's Magic Fountain" (1949). His last movie was "Carrie" in 1952. He has a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame at 7042 Hollywood Boulevard.
