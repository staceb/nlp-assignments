Sergio Renán

Sergio Renán (30 January 1933 – 13 June 2015) was an Argentine actor, movie director and screenwriter. He is known for his movies "Manon" (1984), "Rigoletto" (1985), "Othello" (1987), "Madame Butterfly" (1989), "Cosi Fan Tutte" (1990), and "The Marriage of Figaro" (1992). He was born in Buenos Aires to a Jewish family.

Renán died from pneumonia at a hospital in Buenos Aires, aged 82.
