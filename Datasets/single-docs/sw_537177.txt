Khuzaima Qutbuddin

Khuzaima Qutbuddin (5 June 1940 â€“ 31 March 2016) was a claimant to the title of 53rd Da'i al-Mutlaq of the Dawoodi Bohras. It is a subgroup within the Mustaali, Ismaili Shia branch of Islam. Qutbuddin was appointed "Da'i Madhun" (mazoon mola) by the Syedna Muhammad Burhanuddin in 1965.

Qutbuddin died on 31 March 2016 in Bakersfield, California from cancer, aged 75.
