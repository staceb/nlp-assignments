Anna-Lisa Frykman

Anna-Lisa Frykman born Anna Elisabet Frykman on 4 January 1889 in Stockholm, Sweden, dead 14 June 1960 in Stockholm, Sweden was a Swedish composer, song lyricist and teacher. One of her most famous works is the children's song "Kungens lilla piga". She also wrote the Christmas song "Julpolskan". She was also active under the pseudonym "Eolus".
