Richard Sacher

Richard Sacher (1 September 1947 â€“ 27 February 2014) was a Czech politician and civil servant. He was the Interior Minister for Czechoslovakia for a year. He served from 1989 through 1990. He was also a member of the federal assembly for two years. In 2000, Sacher ran for the senate, but lost the election.

Sacher died on 27 February 2014 in Prague, Czech Republic, aged 71.
