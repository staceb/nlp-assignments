The Crossing Guard

The Crossing Guard is an independent 1995 American drama movie. Jack Nicholson plays Freddy. Anjelica Huston plays Mary. They are a divorced couple whose daughter was killed by a drunk driver. When Freddy finds out that the man who killed his daughter has been released from prison, he decides to kill him. This movie was released in November 1995. It got very positive marks from the critics.
