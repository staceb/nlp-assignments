Sonja Sutter

Sonja Sutter (17 January 1931 â€“ 1 June 2017) was a German movie and television actress. She was one of the few actors that was allowed to appear in productions in both East and West Germany. She was born in Freiburg, Germany.

Sutter was remembered for her role as Fraulein Rottenmeier in the German TV series "Heidi" and for her several roles in the TV series "Derrick" from 1983 to 1998.

Sutter died on 1 June 2017 in Baden bei Wien, Austria at the age of 86.
