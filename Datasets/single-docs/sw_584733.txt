Witness (Katy Perry album)

Witness is the fifth studio album by American singer-songwriter Katy Perry. It is going to be released on June 9, 2017 by Capitol Records. On February 10, 2017, Perry released the single "Chained to the Rhythm", which reached the top five in many countries worldwide, including the US, UK, and Canada. It features the grandson of Bob Marley, Skip Marley, and was co-written by Australian singer-songwriter Sia. Two months later, the single "Bon Appétit" was made available for download. The hip-hop group Migos is featured on this song.

In May 2017, Perry announced that her fifth studio album would be released on June 9 and is called "Witness".

The album received mixed reviews. It earned 180,000 copies sold and streamed in its first week in the United States and became her third number-one album there.

Perry also announced a concert tour called "Witness: The Tour", set to begin in September 2017 across North America.
