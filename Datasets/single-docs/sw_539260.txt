Sir John Leslie, 4th Baronet

Captain Sir John Leslie, 4th Baronet (John Norman Ide Leslie, 6 December 1916 â€“ 18 April 2016), known locally as Jack Leslie, was an Irish aristocrat and media personality.

He was the eldest son of Shane Leslie (Sir John Randolph Leslie, 3rd Baronet) and Marjorie Ide. He became the fourth baronet when his father died in 1971. He was the owner of Castle Leslie in Glaslough.

Leslie died at the age of 99 in Ireland on 18 April 2016.
