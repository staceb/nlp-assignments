James Sloyan

James Sloyan (born February 20, 1940 in Indianapolis, Indiana) is an American actor. He has acted in the television shows "Star Trek" and "Hawaii 5-O". He acted in the movie "The Sting". He has also acted in soap operas.

Sloyan is married and has two children, Daniel and Samantha. In 1957, he received a scholarship to the American Academy of Dramatic Arts. He is married to actress Deirdre Lanihan.
