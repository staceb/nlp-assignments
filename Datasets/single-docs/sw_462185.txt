Loxstedt

Loxstedt is a municipality in Cuxhaven district, Germany. 
There are 21 villages in the municipality.
On 31 December 2012, 15,841 people lived there.

Loxstedt has a railway station. The Autobahn A27 runs through it. The Weser tunnel also is in the area of Loxstedt, near the village of Dedesdorf.
