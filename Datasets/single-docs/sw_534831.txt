Jean-Bernard Raimond

Jean-Bernard Raimond (6 February 1926 â€“ 7 March 2016) was a French politician. He served as Foreign Minister during the government of Jacques Chirac. Raimond was born in Paris. He served from 1986 to 1988. He was the French ambassador to a number of states from the 1970s to the 1990s. He also served as a deputy in the French National Assembly from 1993 to 2002.

Raimond died in Neuilly-sur-Seine, France on 7 March 2016. He was aged 90.
