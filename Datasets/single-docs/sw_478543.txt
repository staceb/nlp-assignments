Mullsjö Secondary School

The Mullsjö Secondary School () was a secondary school in Mullsjö, Sweden. It was opened for the 1997-1998 school year. The last years the school acted as a boarding school for young floorball players.

The school also won the Boys' Floorball World Championship in the years of 2007, 2009 and 2011.

On 26 October 2010 the Mullsjö Municipal Council voted to close down the school after the 2010-2011 school year.
