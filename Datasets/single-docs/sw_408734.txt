Lois Wilson

Lois Wilson (28 June 1894 â€“ 3 March 1988) was an American actress. She appeared in silent movies. She played Molly Wingate in "The Covered Wagon" in 1923 and as Daisy Buchanan in "The Great Gatsby" in 1926. In the 1930s, she became dissatisfied with the roles she was given. She retired from acting in movies in 1941.

Wilson was born in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. She died from pneumonia in Reno, Nevada at 93.
