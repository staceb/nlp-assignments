2 Become 1

"2 Become 1" is a pop ballad by the girl group Spice Girls. The song lyrics talk about the bonding of two lovers. They also talk about the importance of contraception. This song was released in December 1996 in the United Kingdom. It became their third consecutive chart-topper there.

In the United States, the song was released in July 1997. "2 Become 1" peaked at #4 on "Billboard" Hot 100.
