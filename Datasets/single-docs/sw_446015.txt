Liv Dommersnes

Liv Dommersnes (née Strømsted 28 September 1922 – 6 April 2014) was a Norwegian actress and reciter of poetry. She was best known for being a member of the group that founded Studioteatret in 1945. She was born in Oslo (then known as Kristiania). She was married to actor Jens Bolling from 1945 to 1952, and to physician Ivar Dommersnes from 1968 until his death in 1994.

Dommersnes died from natural causes on 6 April 2014 in Oslo. She was 91 years old.
