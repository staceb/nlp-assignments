Tayap

Tayap is a small village in Cameroon. It is from Yaound√©, the capital city. It is from Douala, the biggest city of the country.

The climate is equatorial. It is usually hot, . It is often wet. There are four different seasons. 

In 2005, 272 people lived in Tayap. The village has a primary school. It has 4 classrooms. 

Most people work in agriculture. They produce manioc, bananas, oranges, mangoes, vegetables, peanuts, cocoa and palm oil.

The village is surrounded by a large forest but deforestation is a major problem in this area.
Since 2011 Tayap focuses on biodiversity and ecology. It tries to develop new activities such as tourism.
