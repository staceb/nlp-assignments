Tor Milde

Tor Milde (14 January 1953 – 15 January 2014) was a Norwegian music journalist and writer. He was a judge on the music competition "Idol" in 2005 and 2006. 

Milde was born in Oslo. He started working in journalism in 1984. He was best known as a music journalist and critic for "Verdens Gang" ("VG").

Milde died after a short illness on the morning of 15 January 2014, aged 61, at the Ullevål University Hospital in his hometown of Oslo. He was survived by his two sons and his girlfriend, Irja Anthi.
