Igor Fesunenko

Igor Fesunenko (January 28, 1933 â€“ April 28, 2016) was a Soviet and Russian journalist and writer. He was a professor at the Moscow State Institute of International Relations. He was born in Orenburg.

From 1975 to 1979, he worked in Portugal. He then worked as a political columnist and host of such programs as "Today in the World", "The Camera Looks Into the World", "International Panorama", "Vremya". In the early 1990s, he worked in Italy. He returned to Moscow in the late 1990s. 

Fesunenko died in Moscow, Russia on April 28, 2016, aged 83.
