Brontothere

Brontotheres are extinct mammals. The Brontotheriidae, also called Titanotheriidae, is a family of extinct mammals belonging to the order Perissodactyla. This is the order that includes horses, rhinoceroses, and tapirs. Superficially, Brontotheres looked rather like rhinos, but they were actually more closely related to horses.
