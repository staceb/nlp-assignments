Thaíde

Thaíde is a Brazilian rapper. He was born in São Paulo, Brazil on November 5, 1967.

In April 2009, he took over the program Manos e Minas, the TV Cultura, leaving the program a year later. Signed to TV Bandeirantes in 2010 and currently presents the program "A Liga" on this same station, along with Lobão, Caze Peçanha, Débora Vilalba and Sophia Reis program" and Metro Black Radio" Metropolitana FM of São Paulo along with Marcela Rocha.
