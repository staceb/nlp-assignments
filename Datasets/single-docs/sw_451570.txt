3 Days to Kill

3 Days to Kill is a French-American action-thriller movie. This movie is about an experienced CIA agent. He works with a team to capture a criminal called "The Albino". Kevin Costner plays Ethan Renner. Amber Heard plays Vivi Delay. Hailee Steinfeld plays Zoey. Connie Nielsen plays Christine.

This movie was released in February 2014 in the United States. It got negative reviews. It cost $28 million to make. It made over $44 million.
