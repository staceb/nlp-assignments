The Avengers (movie)

The Avengers is a 2012 American superhero action movie. It is based on Marvel Comics characters. It is distributed under Walt Disney Pictures. "The Avengers" premiered on April 11, 2012. Released in North America on May 4, 2012. The movie was written and directed by Joss Whedon.
