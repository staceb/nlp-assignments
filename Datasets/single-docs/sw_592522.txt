Sangul Utindaan

Sangul-UtindaanË A customary and Spiritual custodian of the people of Sangul in Northern Ghana;eg the current Utindaan is Wunikpel Mbaliye Jato. The descendants of this role are Gasetiib a Clan in Tatale-Sangul District. This cannot be combined with a similar name in the Saboba District of Northern Ghana which has a different title and role.
