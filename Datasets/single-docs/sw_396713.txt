Hanne Sørvaag

Hanne Margrethe Sørvaag (December 27, 1979) is a Norwegian singer and songwriter. She later moved to Stockholm, Sweden. She released her first studio record "You Know Me" under the name Paris in 2002. That gave her three hits in Norway. Her second record, "Talk of the Town", was released in 2006 under her own name. She also writes songs for other musicians.
