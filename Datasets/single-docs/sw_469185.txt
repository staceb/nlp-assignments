Rick Rosas

Rick ("Rick the Bass Player") Rosas (September 10, 1949 â€“ November 6, 2014) was an American musician. He has played with Neil Young, Joe Walsh, Crosby, Stills, Nash & Young, Jerry Lee Lewis, Johnny Rivers, Ron Wood, Etta James, and the short-lived reunion of the Buffalo Springfield, among others. Rosas was born in Los Angeles, California.

Rosas died on November 6, 2014 in Los Angeles, California from lung disease. He was 65.
