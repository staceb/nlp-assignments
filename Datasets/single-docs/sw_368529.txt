Seann William Scott

Seann William Scott (born October 3, 1976) is an American actor. He is best known for playing the role of Steve Stifler in the "American Pie" series. He also played roles in the movies "Final Destination", "Road Trip", "Dude, Where's My Car?", "Evolution", "Bulletproof Monk", "The Rundown", "The Dukes of Hazzard", "Role Models", "Cop Out", and "Goon". Scott voiced the character Crash in the "Ice Age" franchise.
