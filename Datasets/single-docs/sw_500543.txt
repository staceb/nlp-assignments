Stevie McCrorie

Stevie McCrorie is a Scottish singer, musician and firefighter. He is best known for winning the fourth series of "The Voice UK" in April 2015. 

McCrorie's first song was called "Lost Stars". It was released on 5 April 2015. He used to be a singer from 2010 until 2012 and again has had a new career since January 2015.
