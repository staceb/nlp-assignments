What I Got

"What I Got" is the first single off American ska punk band Sublime's third and final studio album "Sublime". The song was released on August 27, 1996.

It is the band's biggest radio hit and was one of the most popular singles of 1997 in the United States. When the song was released, it was able to reach #1 on the Modern Rock Tracks chart and was also a mainstream Top 40 hit, reaching #29 on the Billboard Hot 100 Airplay. The song was ranked #83 in "Rolling Stone"'s "100 Greatest Guitar Songs of All Time".

The song was featured in the 2010 video game "Rock Band 3", the 2011 video game "", the 2000 video game "Dave Mirra Freestyle BMX", and the 2009 video game "Guitar Hero 5".
