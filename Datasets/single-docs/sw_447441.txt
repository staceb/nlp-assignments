Sonia Silvestre

Sonia Margarita Silvestre Ortiz (16 August 1952 – 19 April 2014) was a Dominican singer and announcer. Her best known songs were "Esta Es Sonia Silvestre" (1974), "Yo Quiero Andar" (1990) and "Verde y Negro" (2007). She was born in San Pedro de Macorís, Dominican Republic.

Silvestre died from complications due to a stroke and two heart attacks on 19 April 2014 in Santo Domingo. She was 61.
