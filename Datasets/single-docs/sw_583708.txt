Shaun Kenny-Dowall

Shaun Kenny-Dowall (born 23 January 1988) is a New Zealand professional rugby league player. He plays for the Newcastle Knights of the National Rugby League (NRL). He also played for the Sydney Roosters from 2008 to 2017. He played more than 200 games and scored over a hundred tries for the Roosters. He won the 2013 premiership with the team. He has also played for the New Zealand national team.

Kenny-Dowall was born in Brisbane, Queensland, Australia. His father, John, won a gold and silver medal at the 2000 Summer Paralympics.
