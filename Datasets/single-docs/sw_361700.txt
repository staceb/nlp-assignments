Eve Brent

Eve Brent (Jean Ann Ewers, September 11, 1929 â€“ August 27, 2011) was a Saturn Award-winning American television, movie, and stage actress. She was known for her roles as Jane in "Tarzan's Fight for Life" and as Elaine Connelly in the 1999 movie "The Green Mile". 

Brent was born in Houston, Texas on September 11, 1929. She was married to Micahel Ashe until his death in 2008. They had a son. Brent died on August 27, 2011 in Sun Valley, California from natural causes. She was 81 years old.
