Sleigh Bells (band)

Sleigh Bells is an American noise pop musical group. The members are Derek E. Miller and Alexis Krauss. The group started in 2008. 

Miller met Krauss in 2008 when he was waiting tables at a Brazilian restaurant.
On May 11, 2010, Sleigh Bells' first album "Treats" was released by Mom+Pop and N.E.E.T. Recordings. It got good reviews. Pitchfork gave it a score of 8.7 out of 10. On February 21, 2012 their second album "Reign of Terror" was released. On October 4, 2013 Sleigh Bells' third album "Bitter Rivals" was released. It did not get reviews as good as "Treats" or "Reign of Terror". On the same day Sleigh Bells started a tour.
