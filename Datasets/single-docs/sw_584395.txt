Yumeji Tsukioka

Yumeji Tsukioka (14 October 1921 â€“ 3 May 2017) was a Japanese movie actress. She has appeared in more than 150 movies between 1940 and 1994. She starred in the movie "The Temptress and the Monk" (1958), which was entered into the 8th Berlin International Film Festival. Her husband was the director Umetsugu Inoue.

Tsukioka died on 3 May 2017 from pneumonia in Tokyo at the age of 95.
