Chic Littlewood

Cecil "Chic" Littlewood (17 November 1930 â€“ 11 January 2015) was a New Zealand television entertainer and actor. He appeared as a regular on "Shortland Street" in the role of Laurie Brasch from 1993 to 1996, and had occasional roles in such series as "Mercy Peak". In 2007 he played Isaac Bulosan in the horror film "30 Days of Night", and had a small role as a security guard in Peter Jackson's remake of "King Kong".

Littlewood was born in London. He moved to New Zealand in 1964. He died in Auckland on 11 January 2015 after a long illness, aged 84.
