Kenne Fant

Kenne Fant (born Carl-Henrik Fant; 1 January 1923 – 30 May 2016) was a Swedish actor, director and writer. He was born in Strängnäs. He was known for his roles as Göran Thome "The Most Beautiful on the Earth" (1948) and as Arne in "Prison" (1949). He directed the movie "Adventures of Nils Holgersson" (1962).

Fant died on 30 May 2016 in Stockholm, Sweden at the age of 93.
