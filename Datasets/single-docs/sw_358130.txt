Dana Hill

Dana Hill (born Dana Lynne Goetz; May 6, 1964-July 15, 1996) was an American voice actress. She has a raspy voice. She starred in television programs and movies, including "Goof Troop" (1992-1993). She died resulting from a stroke that was due to having type 1 diabetes (she was diagnosed at age 10). The actress was born around California.
