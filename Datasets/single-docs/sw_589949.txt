Paul Hardin III (June 11, 1931 â€“ July 1, 2017) was an American academic administrator. He was the chancellor of the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill from 1988 to 1995, president of Wofford College from 1968 to 1972, of Southern Methodist University from 1972 to 1974, and of Drew University from 1974 to 1988. 

Hardin died at his Chapel Hill home of ALS-related complications on July 1, 2017, aged 86.
