Gösta Ekman

Hans Gösta Gustaf Ekman (28 July 1939 – 1 April 2017) was a Swedish actor. He was best known for his roles in "Martin Beck" and "Jönssonligan" movies. He began acting in the 1950s.

Ekman was born in Stockholm. His father and grandfather were also actors. He was married three times.

Ekman died on 1 April 2017 in Stockholm, at the age of 77.
