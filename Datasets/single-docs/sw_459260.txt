Cristina Deutekom

Cristina Deutekom, also known as "Christine Deutekom" and "Christina Deutekom" (28 August 1931 – 7 August 2014) was a Dutch coloratura soprano opera singer. Deutekom's stage career ended in 1986, when she suffered a heart attack.

She sang with all the leading tenors of her time, including Carlo Bergonzi, José Carreras, Franco Corelli, Plácido Domingo, Nicolai Gedda, Alfredo Kraus, Luciano Pavarotti, and Richard Tucker.

Deutekom was born in 1931 in Amsterdam as Christine (Stientje) Engel. Her career began in 1963.

She died on 7 August 2014 after a fall in her home.
