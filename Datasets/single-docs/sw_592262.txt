Hrvoje Šarinić

Hrvoje Šarinić (; 17 February 1935 – 21 July 2017) was a Croatian politician. He served as the Prime Minister of the Republic of Croatia from 1992 until 1993. After the parliamentary elections of 1992 he, as member of Croatian Democratic Union (HDZ), was appointed to the post of prime minister.

Šarinić died on 21 July 2017 in Zagreb of cardiopulmonary arrest at the age of 82.
