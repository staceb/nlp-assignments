Jude Law

David Jude Heyworth Law (born 29 December 1972) is a English actor. He was born in Lewisham, London and grew up in Blackheath, London. He played major roles many movies. These include "The Talented Mr. Ripley", "Enemy at the Gates", "A.I. Artificial Intelligence", "Road to Perdition", "Cold Mountain", "Alfie", "Closer" and "The Holiday". 

For his work in "The Talented Mr. Ripley", he won 2000 BAFTA Award for "Actor in a Supporting Role".
