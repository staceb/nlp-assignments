Narendra Modi

Narendra Damodardas Modi (born 17 September 1950) is an Indian politician. He is the current Prime Minister of India serving since 2014. He was the 14th Chief Minister of the state of Gujarat. Modi was elected Prime Minister of India in May 2014. He is a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP). Modi is a Hindu Nationalist. He is a member of the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS).
