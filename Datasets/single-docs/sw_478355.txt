Love Will Never Do (Without You)

"Love Will Never Do (Without You)" is a 1990 hit single from Janet Jackson. The song was Jackson's fifth #1 single on the "Billboard" Hot 100. The song was from her record "Rhythm Nation 1814". The song was released in October 1990.

The song reached #19 on the "Billboard" Year-End Hot 100 of 1991. It was certified gold by the Recording Industry Association of America.
