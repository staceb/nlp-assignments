András Kovács

András Kovács (20 June 1925 – 11 March 2017) was a Hungarian movie director and screenwriter. He directed 30 movies between 1961 and 1996. 

His 1968 movie "The Lost Generation" was entered into the 6th Moscow International Film Festival. His 1978 movie "A ménesgazda" was entered into the 29th Berlin International Film Festival. 

His 1981 movie "Temporary Paradise" won the Silver Prize at the 12th Moscow International Film Festival. His 1985 movie "The Red Countess" was entered into the 14th Moscow International Film Festival.

Kovács was also a member of the jury at the 5th Moscow International Film Festival in 1967 and the 1976 Cannes Film Festival.

Kovács died in Budapest, Hungary on 11 March 2017, aged 91.
