Chris Gayle

Christopher Henry Gayle (born 21 September 1979) is a Jamaican cricketer. He played for the Worcestershire Country Cricket Club, the Western Warriors, and the Kolkata Knight Riders. He now plays for the Royal Challenger Bangalore in the Indian Premier League. In the Big Bash League, Gayle represents the Sydney Thunder. In the Bangladesh Premier League, he represents the Barisal Burners.
