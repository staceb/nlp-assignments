Aziz Ansari

Aziz Ansari (born February 23, 1983) is an American actor and comedian. He is best known for his role as Tom Haverford on the NBC television program "Parks and Recreation". He is also a movie actor, appearing in "Funny People" (2009), "I Love You, Man" (2009), "Observe and Report" (2009), and "30 Minutes or Less" (2011).

Ansari was born in Columbia, South Carolina. He is the son of an Indian Tamil Muslim family. He started acting after graduating from the New York University in 2004. He lives in Los Angeles, California and is an atheist.
