Miranda Hart

Miranda Katharine Hart-Dyke (born 14 December 1972) is a British actress, writer and comedienne. She was born in Torquay, Devon. Her parents are Diana Margaret Luce and David Hart Dyke. Miranda grew up in Petersfield, Hampshire. She writes, produces and stars in the television sitcom "Miranda". Her other acting roles include: "Hyperdrive", "Not Going Out" and "Call the Midwife".
