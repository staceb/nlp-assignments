I Wanna Be Down

"I Wanna be Down" is a 1994 major hit song by Brandy Norwood. The single is about her flirting with a boy, whom she tries to show that she is sexy. The song was released in September 1994.

The song hit #6 on "Billboard" Hot 100. It also hit #1 on "Billboard" Hot R&B Singles Chart for four weeks.
