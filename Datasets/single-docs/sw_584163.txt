Mark Colvin

Mark Colvin (13 March 1952 – 11 May 2017) was a British-born Australian journalist and broadcaster. He was the presenter of "PM"—one of the main Australian radio programs on the ABC Radio network—from 1997 to 2017. He was born in London. He was also known for his works in "The World Today", "Four Corners", "Foreign Correspondent", "7.30 Report" and "Lateline".

Colvin died of lung cancer at a hospital in Sydney on 11 May 2017 at the age of 65.
