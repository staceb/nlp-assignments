Jimmy Roberts

James Wilfred "Jimmy" Roberts (April 9, 1940 â€“ October 23, 2015) was a Canadian ice hockey defenceman and forward. He went by both nicknames of Jimmy and Jim. Roberts played for the Montreal Canadiens and for the St. Louis Blues. He won two Stanley Cups during his career. He retired in 1978 after a twenty year career.

Roberts died on October 23, 2015 of cancer in St. Louis, Missouri at the age of 75.
