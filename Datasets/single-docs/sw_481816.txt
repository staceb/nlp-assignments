Sea Life Minnesota Aquarium

Sea Life Minnesota Aquarium is a public aquarium located in the Mall of America in Bloomington, Minnesota, USA. It holds over 10,000 aquatic creatures. This includes a large collection of sharks, sea turtles, stingrays and fresh water fish. The aquarium also features sea turtles, octopuses, and lobsters. Freshwater exhibits include game fish from the northern United States. There are several giant catfish and a alligator gar. The aquarium has 30 tanks. The aquarium includes a clear acrylic tunnel. It has the "world's largest jellyfish collection" and the "world's largest underground shark exhibit".
