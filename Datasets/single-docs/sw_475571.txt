GMC Acadia

GMC Acadia is a full-sized crossover vehicle. It is manufactured by GMC. It went on sale beginning in December 2006. It seats seven or eight passengers, depending on the model. The GMC Acadia replaces four vehicles the Envoy SUV and its XL package, the Isuzu Asscender SUV and the Pontiac Montana SV6 minivan.

The GMC Acadia was improved in 2012 for the 2013 model year. The second generation Acadia uses the body shell of GM's discontinued Saturn Outlook.
