Homer vs. Lisa and the 8th Commandment

"Homer vs. Lisa and the 8th Commandment" is the thirteenth episode of the second season of "The Simpsons". It first aired on February 7, 1991. In this episode, Homer gets an illegal cable hook-up. despite their enjoyment of the new channels, Lisa becomes that they are stealing cable. Lisa protests by no longer watching television. Meanwhile, Homer invites his friends over to watch a boxing match, but Lisa's protest gets to him. He decides not to watch the fight and cuts the cable. It is one of a number of "The Simpsons" episodes that have won an Emmy.
