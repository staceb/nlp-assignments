Hywel Bennett

Hywel Thomas Bennett (8 April 1944 – 25 July 2017) was a Welsh actor. Bennett was known for his recurring title role as James Shelley in the television sitcom "Shelley" (1979–84), and its sequel "The Return of Shelley" (1988–92).

After becoming known for his role in the comedy movie "The Virgin Soldiers" (1969), Bennett made appearances in movier such as "Loot" (1970) and "Percy" (1971). He was in "EastEnders" during 2003 as gangster Jack Dalton.

Bennett died on 25 July 2017 at the age of 73.
