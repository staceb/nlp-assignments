Jeff Conine

Jeffrey Guy Conine (born June 27, 1966 in Tacoma, Washington) is a former Major League Baseball player. He played for the Florida Marlins, Kansas City Royals, and the Baltimore Orioles. He was in the 1994 All-Star game. He was called Mr. Marlin for his long history with the team.

Conine was a pitcher when he played college ball at UCLA. He was drafted by the Royals as a first baseman. When he joined the Marlins in 1992 he became a left fielder.
