Ian Ross

Ian 'Roscoe' Ross (24 June 194030 April 2014) was an Australian television news presenter. He worked in "Seven News" in Sydney and for the "National Nine News". Ross was born in New South Wales, Australia.

Ross died on the Gold Coast, Queensland, Australia from pancreatic cancer, aged 73. He was divorced from his wife, by whom he had three children and he was in a relationship with a man and was bisexual. He is survived by his partner and children.
