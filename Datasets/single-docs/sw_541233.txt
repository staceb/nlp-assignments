Isao Tomita

, often known simply as Tomita, was a Japanese music composer. He was born in Tokyo. He is called as one of the pioneers of electronic music and space music. He is one of the most famous producers of analog synthesizer arrangements. Many of his albums are electronic versions and adaptations of famous classical music pieces and he received four Grammy Award nominations for his 1974 album "Snowflakes Are Dancing".

Isao died of heart failure in Tokyo on 5 May 2016, aged 84.
