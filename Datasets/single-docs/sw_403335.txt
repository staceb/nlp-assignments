Vicente Aguilar Carmona

Vicente Aguilar Carmona (born April 10, 1970 in Valencia) is a 5-per-team football player from Spain. He has a disability: he is blind. He played 5-per-team football at the 2004 Summer Paralympics. His team finished third after they played Greece and, won 2-0. 

He was a member of the national team in 2013 and competed in the European Championships.
