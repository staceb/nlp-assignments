Malachi Throne

Malachi Throne (December 1, 1928 â€“ March 13, 2013) was an American actor. He was known for his role as False-Face in "Batman" and as Noah Bain in "It Takes a Thief". He had a small role in Steven Spielberg's 2002 movie "Catch Me If You Can".

Throne was born on December 1, 1928 in New York City, New York. He studied at Brooklyn College. His acting career began when he was ten years old. 

Throne was married to Judith Merians from 1965 until they divorced in 1992. They had two children. Then he was married to Marjorie Throne from 1992 until his death in 2013. 

Throne died on March 13, 2013 in his home in Brentwood, Los Angeles, California from lung cancer, aged 84.His remains were cremated.
