Uri Coronel

Maurice Uriel "Uri" Coronel (24 December 1946 in Amsterdam â€“ 18 July 2016 in Amsterdam) was the chairman of Dutch football club Ajax from 2008 through 2011.

Coronel was the person behind the move from De Meer to the current stadium named Amsterdam Arena. In 2007 he was the head commissioner of an investigation to the club's functioning.

Coronel was of Jewish descent. He died in the summer of 2016 at the age of 69.
