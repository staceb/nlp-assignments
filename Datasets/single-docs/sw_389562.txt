Classic NES Series

The Classic NES Series (Famicom Mini in Japan and NES Classics in Europe and Australia) are a series of video games originally released for the Nintendo Entertainment System (the Famicom in Japan) re-released for the Game Boy Advance in 2004 and 2005. They were released for the 20th anniversary of the Famicom in Japan. 12 games were released in the United States, Europe, and Australia, while 30 games were released in Japan. The games released include "Super Mario Bros.", "The Legend of Zelda", and "Pac-Man". A special Game Boy Advance SP which looks like a NES controller was released to go along with the games.
