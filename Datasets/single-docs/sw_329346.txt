Gacé

Gacé is a commune of the Orne in Lower Normandy, France. There are 2,192 people living there in 2012. It was at first, a Gallic city named "*Vassiākon". The Castle of Gacé was built in the 11th century. The English captured the castle in 1417.

The city had important economic activity in the beginning of 19th century. It's a industrial and agricultural town.

There is the Museum of Lady of the Camellias.
