Nicktoons Basketball

Nicktoons Basketball is a video game for the PC, released in 2004. It is based on a bunch Nickelodeon and Nick Jr. shows. It is the third Nicktoon game to be released by ImaginEngine, and also the first by Digital Eclipse.

The player plays basketball with SpongeBob SquarePants, Timmy Turner, , Danny Phantom, Tommy Pickles, Dora the Explorer ,and more. The player can choose how they would like to control the game as well.
