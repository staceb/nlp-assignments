Uche Chukwumerije

Uche Chukwumerije (November 1939 â€“ 19 April 2015) was a Nigerian politician for the People's Democratic Party (PDP). He represented the Abia North Senatorial District from 29 May 2003 until his death.

Chukwumerije was born in Abia State to a Christian family. One of his sons is Chika Chukwumerije (born 1983), a taekwondo artist and Olympic medalist.

Chukwumerije died from lung cancer on 19 April 2015 in Abuja. He was 75.
