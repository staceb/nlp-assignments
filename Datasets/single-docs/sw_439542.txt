Georgette Rejewski

Georgette Alice Victorine Henriette Reijevsky (12 February 1910 â€“ 21 February 2014), better known by her stage name Georgette Rejewski, was a Belgian-Dutch actress. She began her career in 1934. She appeared in the movies "Any Day Now" (1976), "A Bridge Too Far" (1977) and "Another Mother" (1996).

She was born in Antwerp. Her father was Russian and her mother was Belgian. She married Dominique Antoine Frederic Rimathe in 1932. He died at the Bergen-Belsen concentration camp in 1945, at the end of World War II.

Rejewski died from natural causes on 21 February 2014 at a in Middenbeemster, North Holland. She was aged 104. She had been living in the nursing home for the last years of her life.
