Laugh Now, Cry Later

Laugh Now, Cry Later is the seventh studio album by American rapper, Ice Cube. "Laugh Now, Cry Later" was released on June 9, 2006 through Lench Mob Records. It was the first album that was released through his independently owned record label. It is also Ice Cube's first studio album in six years. 

The album debuted at #4 on the "Billboard" 200. "Laugh Now, Cry Later" was certified Gold by the RIAA which is uncommon for an album that was released through an independent label.
