Bootlegs &amp; B-Sides

Bootlegs & B-Sides is a 1994 compilation studio album by Ice Cube. This album has remixes and b-sides for certain songs, including "Check Yo Self" and "What Can I Do". Other songs on the album are from his album "Lethal Injection".

This album was released on November 22, 1994. Many song versions were later included on remastered and proper Ice Cube records.
