Pat O'Brien (personality)

Patrick John "Pat" O'Brien (born February 14, 1948) is an American author and radio host. He was best known for his work as a sportscaster with CBS Sports from 1981 to 1997, as well as his work as the anchor and host of "Access Hollywood" from 1997 to 2004, and "The Insider" from 2004 to 2008.
