Espresso

Espresso is a type of Italian coffee that is concentrated. In order to make espresso, coffee beans are powdered and hot water is added under high pressure. Because espresso is so strong, it is usually mixed with other coffee drinks in small amounts.

There are many drinks that use espresso, such as latte, cappuccino, caffe mocha and caffe macchiato.
