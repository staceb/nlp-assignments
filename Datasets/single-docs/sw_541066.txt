Ángel de Andrés López

Ángel de Andrés López (23 October 1951 – 4 May 2016) was a Spanish actor. He appeared in more than 60 movies and television shows between 1977 and 2015. He was known for his roles as Antonio in "What Have I Done to Deserve This?" (1984) and as Cheyenne in "800 Bullets (2002).

On 4 May 2016, Andrés López died at his home in Miraflores de la Sierra, Madrid, Spain from a heart attack, aged 64.
