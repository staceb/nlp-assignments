James Brolin

James Brolin (born Craig Kenneth Bruderlin, July 18, 1940) is an American movie, television, voice, stage actor, movie producer, and movie director. He is known for his roles in "Love, Wedding, Marriage", "Westworld", "Capricorn One", "The Car", and in "The Amityville Horror". He has won a Golden Globe Award and an Emmy Award.

Brolin was born on July 18, 1940 in Los Angeles, California. He studied at University High School. Brolin was married to Jane Cameron Agee from 1966 until they divorced in 1984. Then he was married to Jan Smithers from 1986 until they divorced in 1995. He has been married to Barbara Streisand since 1998. He has three children, including actor Josh Brolin.
