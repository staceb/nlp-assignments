Barbara McNair

Barbara Jean McNair (born 4 March 1934 â€“ 4 February 2007) was an American musician and actress. She became very popular in the 1960s. She was a guest on many television programs, including "The Bell Telephone Hour", "The Steve Allen Show" and "The Hollywood Palace". Her Broadway play credits included "No Strings" and "The Body Beautiful" in the 1960s and 1970s.

McNair was born in Chicago. Around her seventies, she lived in Los Angeles. She died there resulting from throat cancer, age seventy-two.
