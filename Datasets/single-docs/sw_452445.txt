Barbara Murray

Barbara Ann Murray (27 September 1929 â€“ 20 May 2014) was an English actress. She was known in movies for appearing in "Passport to Pimlico" (1949), "Meet Mr. Lucifer" (1953) and "The Curse of King Tut's Tomb" (1980). On television, she was best known for her role as Lady Pamela Wilder in the 1960s drama series "The Plane Makers" (and the sequel, "The Power Game"). She was born in London.

Murray was married to actor John Justin from 1952 until they divorced in 1964. The couple had three daughters.

Murray died from a heart attack in Spain, aged 84. She had been in hospital for an operation to fix her broken hip.
