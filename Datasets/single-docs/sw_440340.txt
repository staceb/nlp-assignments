Inge Keller

Inge Keller (15 December 1923 – 6 February 2017) was a German actress. Her career on stage and on movies has for seventy years. She was one of the most well-known performers in the former German Democratic Republic. Keller was known for her roles in "The Last Year", "Aimée & Jaguar", "Council of the Gods", "Lola and Billy the Kid", "Karla", "The Fiancé", and in "Woman Doctors".

Keller was born on 15 December 1923 in Berlin, Germany. Keller died in a Berlin nursing home on 6 February 2017, aged 93.
