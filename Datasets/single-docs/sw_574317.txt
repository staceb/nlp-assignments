Robin Stenuit

Robin Stenuit (born June 16, 1990 in Ottignies-Louvain-la-Neuve) is a Belgian road bicycle racer. His speciality is sprinting.

Participating in professional teams since 2011, Stenuit has been riding for Wanty-Groupe Gobert since 2016.

He achieved several victories, among others the Grand Prix de la ville de Nogent-sur-Oise and the Memorial Van Coningsloo, both in 2015.
