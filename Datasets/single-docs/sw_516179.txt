Moti Kirschenbaum

Mordechai "Moti" Kirschenbaum (; September 24, 1939 â€“ September 25, 2015) was an Israeli media personality and documentarian. He was born in Kfar Saba. 

He was a staff member of the Israeli television from its first day in May 1968, and was editor of "Mabat", its news program, during its first three years. He directed several documentaries, and produced and edited several television programs (including "Lo HaKol Over" and "Nikui Rosh").

Kirschenbaum died in Mikhmoret at the age of 76.
