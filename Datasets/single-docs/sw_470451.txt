Trophy Wife

Trophy Wife is an American television sitcom. It aired during the 2013–14 television season on ABC. The series was co-created and executive produced by Emily Halpern and Sarah Haskins for ABC Studios. The series was green-lit by ABC for a series order pick up on May 10, 2013. "Trophy Wife" premiered on September 24, 2013.

On May 8, 2014, ABC canceled "Trophy Wife" after one season. The final episode aired the following week. 

The series starred Malin Åkerman, Bradley Whitford and Marcia Gay Harden.
