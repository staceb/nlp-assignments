Claude Fagedet

Claude Fagedet (30 March 1928 â€“ 26 June 2017) was a French photographer. He took many photographs of Occitanie, including his birthplace of Narbonne and the chalets in Gruissan. He also took photographs on his many trips abroad. 

Fagedet was awarded the Meilleur Ouvrier de France.

Fagedet died in Narbonne, France on 26 June 2017, aged 89.
