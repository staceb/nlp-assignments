Nyhem Week

The Nyhem Week () is a major annual conference by the Swedish Pentecostal Movement. It occurs during the Midsummer week every year. The conference is held in Nyhem in Mullsjö Municipality in Sweden. It was first held in 1917. Host is the Mullsjö-Nyhem Pentecostal Congregation.

Up to 2008 the conference were held inside a large tent. From 2009 the conference is instead held inside Nyhemshallen.
