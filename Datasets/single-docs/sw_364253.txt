Jill Arrington

Tiffany "Jill" Arrington (born July 27, 1972) is an American sportscaster. She works for Fox Sports. 

Arrington was the feature reporter on "Fox NFL Sunday" and co-hosted "NFL Under the Helmet". For the Arena Football League, she was the studio host and field reporter for the TNN Network. Arrington also worked at CBS Sports. She was the sideline reporter and court reporter for "Tennis on CBS" and "SEC on CBS" and was a feature reporter on "The NFL Today" a pregame show for the "NFL on CBS". Then she worked with Mike Tirico and Kirk "Herbie" Herbstreit for "ESPN College Football" as lead sideline reporter. Arrington has hosted programs for FOX Sports such as "Totally NASCAR", "Verizon NFL Mobile Updates", "College Football Saturday Preview," and "FoxSportsFlash". She was added to the "NBA on TNT" in 2012.

Arrington was born in Conyers, Georgia. She studied Journalism and Political Science at the University of Miami. In 2001, Arrington was voted the "Sexiest Sportscaster" by "Playboy" magazine. Arrington is the aunt of actresses Dakota and Elle Fanning.
