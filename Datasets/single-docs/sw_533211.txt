Peter Lustig

Peter Fritz Willi Lustig (October 27, 1937 – February 23, 2016) was a German television presenter and author of children's books. He was known as leading actor in the weekly children's television show "Löwenzahn". He hosted the show from 1979 up until 2006; during its first year the show was still called "Pusteblume"). He also appeared on the shows "Mittendrin" (1987–1995) and "Gordos Reise ans Ende der Welt" (2007).

Lustig died on February 23, 2016 at his home in Bohmstedt, Germany. He died from stomach cancer. He was aged 78. He was surrounded by his family. He is survived by four children and nine grandchildren.
