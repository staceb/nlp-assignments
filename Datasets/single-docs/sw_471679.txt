Óscar Humberto Mejía Victores

Brigadier General Óscar Humberto Mejía Victores (December 9, 1930 – February 1, 2016) was a Guatemalan politician. He was the 27th President of Guatemala from 8 August 1983 to 14 January 1986. 

Victores died in Guatemala City from Parkinson's disease on February 1, 2016 at the age of 85.
