Máel Muire ni Cináeda

Máel Muire ni Cináeda was the fourth child of Kenneth MacAlpin and an unknown wife. She was born in 842. She married Áed Findliath, high king of Ireland. They had 4 children. One of their children later became high king of Ireland. Máel also married another High king of Ireland but had no children with him. She died in either 912 or according to the Annals of Ulster died in 913. There are no known depictions of her.
