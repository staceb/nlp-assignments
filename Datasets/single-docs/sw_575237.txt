Carl Adam Lewenhaupt

Carl Adam Göstasson ""Noppe"" Lewenhaupt (1 August 1947 – 28 February 2017) was a Swedish count, businessman and restaurateur. 

Lewenhaupt worked at a bank and warehouse in London and New York. In the mid 1990s, he created and started Geddesholm Callcenter, one of Swedens largest call-centers, better known as Avesta teletjänst, which he sold in 2002. The same year he along with Erik Lallerstedt started the members only club "Noppe" at Östermalm in Stockholm.

Lewenhaupt was found dead on 28 February 2017 in a river outside Gärdet in Stockholm.
