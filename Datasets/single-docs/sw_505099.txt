Ali Raymi

Ali Raymi (7 December 1973 â€“ 23 May 2015) was a Yemeni boxer. He was a four division flyweight Boxing World Champion. His professional boxing record was 26-0 (26 KOs), twenty of which happened in the first round versus undefeated fighters. He was a Yemeni amateur champion in 2004, 2005, 2006 and 2007. He was born in Mecca, Saudi Arabia.

Raymi died in an explosion on 23 May 2015 in Sana'a, aged 41.
