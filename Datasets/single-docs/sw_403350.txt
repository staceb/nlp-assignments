Marcelo Rosado Carrasco

Marcelo Rosado Carrasco (born October 2, 1978 in MÃ¡laga) is a 5-per-team football player from Spain. He has a disability: he is blind and is a B1 type sportperson. He played 5-per-team football at the 2004 Summer Paralympics. His team finished third after they played Greece and, won 2-0. He played 5-per-team football at the 2012 Summer Paralympics. His team finished third after they played Argentina and, won 1-0. 

He was a member of the national team in 2013 and competed in the European Championships.
