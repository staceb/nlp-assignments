Elon Gold

Elon Gold (born September 14, 1970) is an American comedian, television actor, writer and producer. Gold was a guest judge on the celebrity impersonation series, "The Next Big Thing". He has been in multiple movies such as "Cheaper by the Dozen". He was also in the sitcom "In-laws".

Gold is from New York. In his younger years, Gold attended the Westchester Day School, in Westchester, and the Marsha Stern Talmudical Academy, in Manhattan. Gold has also stated that he is an observing Jew.
