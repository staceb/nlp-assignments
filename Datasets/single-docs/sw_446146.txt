Erzsi Kovács

Erzsi Kovács DRH (2 June 1928 – 6 April 2014) was a Hungarian pop singer and performer. 

She made her last album, "Mosolyogva búcsúzom" (English title: "I Leave with a Smile") when she was 79 years old. She was awarded the highest honor in Hungary, the Order of Merit of the Republic of Hungary.

She died after long illness on 6 April 2014.
