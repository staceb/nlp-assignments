The Billionaire of Dismal Downs

"Tbe Billionaire of Dismal Downs" is the ninth episode of "The life and times of Scrooge McDuck".

After finding gold, Scrooge buys the Whitehorse Bank and begins to build a financial empire. Scrooge goes back to Scotland where he tries to prove that he is a "real Scotsman" by playing in the "Scottish Games". Scrooge fails but he decides to install his matrices in Duckburg, on the Occidental coast. The episode ends with Scrooge and his sisters going to Duckburg.
