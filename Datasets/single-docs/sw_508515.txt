Rebel Heart

Rebel Heart is the thirteenth studio album by American singer-songwriter Madonna. It was her second album to be released under Interscope Records, after her previous album, "MDNA". It was released on March 10, 2015. Rebel Heart debuted at number two on the US "Billboard" 200. It contained the singles "Living for Love" and "Bitch I'm Madonna". The second song, "Bitch I'm Madonna", features Trinidadian-American rapper Nicki Minaj. American disc jockey Diplo was one of the producers of the album, along with Madonna herself.
