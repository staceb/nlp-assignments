Janine Sutto

Janine Sutto (20 April 1921 – 28 March 2017) was a French-born Canadian actress. She was born in Paris, but raised in Montreal. In 1943, she was a founding member of the Théâtre de l'Equipe troupe and continued acting on stage through the early 1950s with Théâtre du Nouveau Monde.

Sutto was known for her roles in "Kamouraska", "Congorama", and in "Route 132".

Sutto died in Montreal on 28 March 2017.
