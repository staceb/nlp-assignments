Toby Smith

Toby Grafftey-Smith (29 October 1970 â€“ 12 April 2017), known professionally as Toby Smith, was an English musician. He was most famous for being the keyboardist and co-songwriter for Jamiroquai from 1992 until he left from the band in 2002.

Smith left Jamiroquai in 2002 during the Funk Odyssey tour due to family commitments.

He was the music producer and manager for the English pop-rock band, The Hoosiers. 
He owned Angelic Recording Studios based near Banbury.

Smith died on 11 April 2017. He was 46 years old.
