Ed Begley

Edward James "Ed" Begley, Sr. (March 25, 1901 â€“ April 28, 1970) was an American movie, television, stage, radio, and voice actor. He won an Academy Award and a Tony Award. He was known for his roles in "The Unsinkable Molly Brown", "Billion Dollar Brain", "The Dunwich Horror", "Hang 'Em High", and in "12 Angry Men".

Begley was born on March 25, 1901 in Hartford, Connecticut. He was married to Amanda Huff from 1922 until they divorced in 1957. Then he was married to Dorothy Reeves from 1961 until they divorced in 1963. He was last married to Helen Jordan from 1963 until his death in 1970. Begley died on April 28, 1970 in his Hollywood, California home from a heart attack, aged 69. His son is actor Ed Begley, Jr..
