Shanna Moakler

Shanna Lynn Moakler (born March 28, 1975) is an American model, actress, and former beauty queen. She won Miss New York USA in 1995. She became Miss USA when Chelsi Smith won Miss Universe. Moakler was the Playmate of the Month for "Playboy" in December 2001.

Moakler was born in Providence, Rhode Island.
